# Mining Game - Game Design Document

## Overview

A top-down mining dungeon crawler where the player descends through underground floors, mining ore and building bots to survive. The player is a miner, not a fighter — combat is handled by bots built from the same ores you're trying to bring home.

## Core Fantasy

You're a miner pushing deeper into dangerous mines. Every ore you dig up is both potential profit and potential survival. Build bots to protect yourself and help you mine, but every bot you build is money you're not taking home.

## Core Pillars

1. **Resource Tension** — Ore is currency AND crafting material. Spending ore on bots means less profit. Hoarding ore means less protection.
2. **Risk vs. Reward** — Deeper floors have better ore but harder enemies. Pushing past checkpoints carries your bots forward but risks your entire ore haul.
3. **You're a Miner, Not a Fighter** — The pickaxe does low damage. Bots are your real defense. Building and placing them smartly is the skill expression.
4. **Accessible Depth** — Simple moment-to-moment gameplay (mine nodes, place bots, go deeper) with layered decision-making underneath.
